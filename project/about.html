Purpose

This Garden Yield Calculator was created to help gardeners plan how much to plant and how much to expect from their harvest. It takes common square foot gardening recommendations and turns them into a simple tool that calculates seeds needed and estimated yield based on the space available.

How the Garden Yield Calculator Works

The calculator uses a list of vegetables with standard seed planting densities from square foot gardening, such as 1, 4, 9, or 16 plants per square foot. When a vegetable is selected and the number of square feet is entered, the tool calculates the total number of seeds needed and estimates the expected yield. Yield values are based on typical production per square foot and are adjusted automatically based on the size of the planting area.
