Helping gardeners plan a bountiful harvest

This Garden Yield Calculator helps you estimate how much to plant and how much you can expect to harvest from your garden space. Start by selecting a vegetable from the list, then enter the number of square feet you plan to grow. The calculator will automatically determine how many seeds you’ll need and estimate your total yield based on typical production per square foot. It will also suggest how your harvest is best stored. Whether in jars, freezer bags, or as fresh or dry produce. Use this tool to plan your planting, reduce waste, and better prepare for storing your harvest.
